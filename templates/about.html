About Billify

Billify is a unique tool designed for music enthusiasts who want to relive the top hits from the past. Our platform scrapes the Billboard Top 100 songs from any week in history and creates a custom Spotify playlist for you. Whether you're feeling nostalgic or just exploring classic tracks, Billify helps you bring those songs back to life.

Simply select a date, and Billify will do the rest, delivering a Spotify playlist filled with the top tracks from that week. Experience the magic of discovering or rediscovering your favorite music with ease!
